Gulab Jamun

Description-

Gulab jamun is a popular South Asian dessert made from khoya (reduced milk solids) or milk powder, shaped into small balls, deep-fried until golden brown, and soaked in a sugar syrup flavored with cardamom, rose water, or saffron. The result is a sweet, soft, and spongy treat, often enjoyed at festivals, celebrations, and special occasions.
